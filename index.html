Hi, I'm Alwin, but most people know me as "themaxpa." I'm an enthusiastic IT student currently pursuing a Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) at Mahatma Gandhi University, specializing in Software Development. As a self-learner, I thrive on exploring new technologies and building projects that challenge and enhance my skills. I’ve gained practical experience through personal projects, including my current work on a Flutter app, which allows me to deepen my understanding of mobile app development. I'm also proficient with Git and GitHub, using them to manage and collaborate on my projects.

I enjoy tackling projects that challenge my problem-solving abilities and allow me to explore innovative solutions. From developing web and mobile applications to exploring the latest in tech, I'm always eager to expand my knowledge and contribute to meaningful projects. When I’m not coding, you’ll probably find me gaming with friends or spending quality time with my family. Feel free to connect with me or check out my portfolio to see what I’ve been working on!
